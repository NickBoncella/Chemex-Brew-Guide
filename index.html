Chemex Brew Guide

  1. Weigh out coffee using a 16:1 water to coffee ratio and begin to preheat water to 203 ℉.
  2. After the water is preheated, prewet the brewer with the filter inserted to remove any papery taste and discard water.
  3. Place the brewer on the scale, put in your freshly ground coffee, and tare the scale to zero.
  4. Your first pour is called the bloom. Pour double the amount of coffee in water and allow the coffee to bloom for 45 seconds.
  5. Next, slowly pour the desired amount of water by the 3 minute mark and allow to drain. Begin the pour in the center and work outward in concentric circles followed by a center pour once the slurry is within a half inch of the top of the brewer.
  6. The total brew and drain time shouldbe around 5 and a half minutes.
  7. Lastly, pour and enjoy!
